Rustic French Bread

Description

I found this recipe one day while scrolling through TikTok. It seemed simple enough so I decided to try it out the next time we made a pasta dish for dinner. This bread is incredibly fast to make and is the perfect side for any soup or sauce-based meal. As an added bonus, we usually spead some butter and garlic on the top when it is fresh out of the oven. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 1 1/4 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 cups of bread flour

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and knead for five minutes. Knead in the bowl for easy cleanup.
  2. Lightly cover with olive oil, cover bowl, and let rise for 45 minutes.
  3. Once risen, roll dough into a long oval shape that is about two hands long. Fold 1/3 of the long side into the middle. Cover the fold with the other 1/3. Pinch any seems together. Shape the ends to form a loaf.
  4. Make three slashes in the top of the loaf and brush with ice water.
  5. Place in a cold oven and turn heat to 400. Bake for 27 minutes.
  6. Let sit for 15 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
