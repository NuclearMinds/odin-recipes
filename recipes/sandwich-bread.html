Homemade Sandwich Bread

Description

This bread is adapted from a recipe from King Arthur Baking Company. Their recipe can be found here. The bread is the perfect bread for a grilled cheese or some toast at breakfast. For a more dense loaf, bake in a cold 350 degree oven. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of all-purpose bread
  • 1/2 cup of milk (1%, 2%, or whole)
  • 1/2 to 2/3 cup of hor water
  • 4 tablespoons of melted butter or 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • 1 packet of active dry yeast or 2 teaspoons of instant yeast

Steps

  1. Combine milk and hot water in a small bowl to make a luke warm mixture. Combine mixture with all other ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Mix until a dough ball forms and does not stick to the bowl. Knead dough for 6 minutes on a greased surface or in a mixer.
  2. Transfer the dough to a lightly greased bowl and let dough rise for 1 to 2 hours until it has risen to just under half the original size.
  3. Gently deflate and form into a loaf on a greased surface. Place loaf in a 8" loaf pan and cover with plastic wrap. Let continue rising for 60 minutes. Towards the end of the rise, preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  4. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, until it is light golden brown. Internal temperature of the loaf should be 190 degrees.
  5. Let cool on rack before slicing. Store in a plastic bag at room temperature for several days.
